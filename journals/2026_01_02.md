- ## Calphad tdb数据库获取
  collapsed:: true
	- ((64465741-07d5-4238-a48f-8dbc8cba938d))
- Pandat需要.tdb数据库绘制相图，除了随软件购买的数据库，还可以导入用户编写的tdb数据库
- 期刊论文中检索：搜索“元素名+Calphad”，在论文附件中寻找 [Al Li Calphad - Google 学术搜索](https://scholar.google.com.hk/scholar?hl=zh-CN&as_sdt=0%2C5&as_vis=1&q=Al+Li++Calphad&btnG=)
- TDBDB索引库：在TDBDB网站输入元素（逗号隔开）查找 [TDB DB](https://avdwgroup.engin.brown.edu/)，网站说明 [avdwgroup.engin.brown.edu/help.html](https://avdwgroup.engin.brown.edu/help.html)
- 商业数据库：[avdwgroup.engin.brown.edu/help.html](https://avdwgroup.engin.brown.edu/help.html)
